RGB is a workshop-based, one-day, educational event held annually at the University of California, Berkeley.

Participants learn about essential design and photography skills, career oppotunities, and philosophies through workshops led by industry professionals.

At the end of the event, every participant is expected to produce a piece of original work that demonstrates the utilization of skills acquired from the workshops. Workshop leaders are invited to provide feedback after participantsâ€™ project presentations and award outstanding pieces. RGB aims to provide learning, practicing, and networking opportunities for Berkeley students and design and photography professionals. We would love to have you join us.
